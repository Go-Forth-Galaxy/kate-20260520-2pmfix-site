The Process

How we work — visualized.

Every engagement is tailored. The shape below is the rhythm we return to — yours becomes its own thing.

01

Assess

We meet the leader where they are. 360 input, business context, and the leader's own honest read on what's hard right now.

02

Anchor

We define the development arc — three to four moves that, if made, change the leader and the business.

03

Coach

Biweekly or weekly sessions, paired with field work. Real situations, real reps, real reflection.

04

Iterate

We check in with the system around the leader — board, peers, team — and adjust the work to what the business is asking next.

05

Compound

Coaching ends; capacity remains. We hand off the practices that will keep the leader growing without us.
